Better Justel – Enable Case Law Abstracts (EXPERIMENTAL)

Before enabling this experimental feature, please read and agree to all three conditions below. The "Enable display of case law" button will become active only once all checkboxes are checked. After clicking the button, you will need to reload open Better Justel pages.

After clicking this button, your browser will ask you to explicitly grant this extension access to raw.githubusercontent.com (the source of the case law data). Case law will only be displayed if you grant that permission.
